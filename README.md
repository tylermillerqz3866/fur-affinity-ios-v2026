## Overview

Fur Affinity is an unofficial app for iPhone and iPad that connects to furaffinity.net and brings a native-style iOS experience to the site. It is implemented with Swift and SwiftUI, with the goal of making it easier to keep up with submissions, notifications, and account activity while away from a desktop browser.

The app is aimed at people in the furry fandom who prefer a dedicated mobile client over the web interface. It covers everyday use cases like opening submissions and journals, reading and posting comments, viewing profiles, and managing follows from a clean iOS layout.

## What it includes

- Submissions feed for browsing recent content
- Notifications feed for staying current with account activity
- Notes view with read-only access
- Submission and journal viewing
- Comment reading and writing support
- User profile browsing
- Follow and unfollow actions
- GIF support, plus sharing and Handoff integration

## How to use it

Once the app is open, sign in with your Fur Affinity account and start with either the submissions feed or the notifications feed. From there, you can open posts, read journals, inspect profiles, and move between content without leaving the app.

Common flows include:
- Open a submission to view its details and media
- Check notifications for new activity
- Read journal entries from users you follow
- Write comments where interaction is supported
- Visit a profile and adjust follow status as needed
- Use sharing or Handoff when moving content between apps or devices
